About Us

The Trillium Lane Homeowners Association exists for one purpose: caring for our shared park, the Meadow. Membership is open to all households in the neighborhood.

The HOA has served the neighborhood since 1971 as a nonprofit mutual benefit corporation registered in California. Annual dues cover the Meadow's property tax, insurance, and routine upkeep — mowing, gardening, and minor repairs — so it stays beautiful and open for everyone to enjoy.

Annual Dues

Dues are collected once a year and go entirely toward maintaining the Meadow. Every household that joins helps keep this shared space healthy.

2026 Dues — $240 per household, payable once a year or monthly with online billing.

  • Property tax on the Meadow
  • Liability insurance
  • Mowing & landscaping
  • Minor repairs and upkeep
Pay Annually — $240/yr Pay Monthly — $20/mo

Prefer to pay by check? Yearly payment by check should be made out to Trillium Lane HOA, and delivered to the President or Treasurer. Receipts available on request.
